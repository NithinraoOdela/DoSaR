More About DoSaR

  • What is our Vision?

    The predominant intention of the department is to make sure the surveillance and registration for each of the Bitsians and non-Bitsians.To make sure that the fest to show up without any disturbances associated with registration and safety issues.

  • Work we do with other Departments?

    The primary agenda of our department with the alternative departments are keeping the point of contact (POC) for any problems associated with the registration, imparting passes to the visitors and sponsors, and problems associated with barricading, etc.

  • Barrication?

    As safety and surveillance being elements of our department, we guarantee that no person trace passes the barricadion. And offer the maximum safety to make certain the protection of the participants, audience, and celebrities who are performing.

  • Our Surveillance

    This is certainly considered one of our important agendas in our department. we make certain that security guards are positioned in the locations in whereever required, there will be guards during each event,at the gates and many others locations in whichever required throughout the fest, we keep monitoring them and offer maximum safety.
